WHO AM I?

My name is MJ Vibar, an aspiring professional musician based in General Trias, Cavite with the love for making and spreading music.

I have been interested in music since my elementary days and have now been aiming to improve my musicality and technical skills. My musical journey started when I was a kid, with a cheap electric piano where I tried playing songs by ear. The instruments that I've been learning include the piano, drums, electric and acoustic guitar, bass guitar, and mainly the saxophone which is my main instrument.
